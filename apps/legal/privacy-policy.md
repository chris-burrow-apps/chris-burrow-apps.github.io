# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 27, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how we ("the Developer") collect, use, and share your information when you use our mobile applications (each, an "Application").

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### 1.1 Personal Data
Our Applications are generally designed to work without requiring you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. Any data you enter within the Application is typically stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers unless specifically stated within the Application's features.

### 1.2 Permissions
The Application may request certain permissions to provide specific features:
- **Audio/Microphone**: Used only if the Application provides voice-input features. Audio is processed for speech-to-text and is not stored or transmitted by the Developer.
- **Internet**: Used to communicate with third-party services for analytics and crash reporting.

### 1.3 Third-Party Services
To improve our Applications and diagnose technical issues, we use the following third-party services:

*   **Google Analytics for Firebase**: Used to understand how users interact with the Application (e.g., which screens are visited). This data is anonymized.
*   **Firebase Crashlytics**: Used to collect crash reports so we can fix bugs and improve stability. These reports contains technical information about your device and the nature of the crash.

## 2. Your Choices (Opt-Out)
You have control over your data. Most of our Applications allow you to enable or disable **Analytics** and **Crash Reporting** at any time via the **Settings** menu.

## 3. Data Storage
Data created within the Application is stored locally on your device using standard Android storage mechanisms. If you delete the Application, this local data will be removed.

## 4. Children’s Privacy
Our Applications do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
